Dumbbell Lunges

Exercise Description

Dumbbell lunges strengthen the legs and glutes. Stand with dumbbells at your sides, step forward with one leg, lower your body until both knees are bent at 90 degrees, then push back to start. Alternate legs.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Weights: 4-5 kg

Rest time between each set: 30secs
