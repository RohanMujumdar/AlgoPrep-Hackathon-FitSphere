Kettlebell Swing

Exercise Description

Kettlebell swings target the core and glutes. Stand with feet hip-width apart, swing a kettlebell between your legs, then drive your hips forward to swing it up to shoulder height.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 7-11 kg

Rest time between each set: 30secs
