Cable Rows

Exercise Description

Cable rows target the back and biceps. Sit at a cable row machine with your feet braced and hands gripping the handle. Pull the handle towards your torso while keeping your back straight, then slowly return to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 10-15 kg per hand

Rest time between each set: 30secs
