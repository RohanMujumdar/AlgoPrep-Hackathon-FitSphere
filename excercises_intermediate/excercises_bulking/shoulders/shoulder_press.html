Shoulder Press

Exercise Description

Sit or stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding dumbbells or a barbell at shoulder height. Press the weights overhead until your arms are fully extended, then slowly lower them back to shoulder height.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 10-15 kg dumbbell per hand

Rest time between each set: 45secs
