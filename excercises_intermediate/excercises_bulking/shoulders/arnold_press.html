Arnold Press

Exercise Description

Start with dumbbells at shoulder height, palms facing you. Press the dumbbells overhead while rotating your palms outward, then lower back to the start while rotating palms back in.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 10-15 kg dumbbell per hand

Rest time between each set: 45secs
