Skull Crusher

Exercise Description

Lie on a bench with a barbell or dumbbells held above your chest, arms extended. Lower the weight towards your forehead by bending your elbows, then extend your arms back to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 10-12kg

Rest time between each set: 45secs
