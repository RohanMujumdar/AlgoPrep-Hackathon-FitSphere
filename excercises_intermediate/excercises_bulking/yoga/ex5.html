Setu Bandhasanas

Exercise Description

Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor, hip-width apart. Lift your hips towards the ceiling while pressing your feet and arms into the ground. Hold the pose, squeezing your glutes and engaging your core.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Rest time between each set: 45secs
