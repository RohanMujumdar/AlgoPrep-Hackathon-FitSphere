Adho Mukha Svanasana

Exercise Description

Start on your hands and knees, with wrists under shoulders and knees under hips. Lift your hips towards the ceiling, straightening your legs and pressing your heels towards the floor. Your body should form an inverted V shape.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Rest time between each set: 45secs
