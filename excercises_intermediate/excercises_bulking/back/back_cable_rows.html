Back Cable Rows

Exercise Description

Sit at a cable row machine with your feet braced and your knees slightly bent. Grip the handle with both hands and pull it towards your torso, squeezing your shoulder blades together, then slowly return to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Weights: 20-25 kg

Rest time between each set: 45secs
