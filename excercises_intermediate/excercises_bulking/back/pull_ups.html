Pull-Ups

Exercise Description

Hang from a pull-up bar with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart and your palms facing away from you. Pull your body up until your chin is above the bar, then lower yourself back down with control. A good warm-up excercise for the back.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest time between each set: 45secs
