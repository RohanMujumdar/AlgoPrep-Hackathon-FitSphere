Leg Press

Exercise Description

Sit on the leg press machine with your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform. Push the platform away by extending your legs, then slowly lower it back down until your knees are at a 90-degree angle.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 20-25kg

Rest time between each set: 45secs
