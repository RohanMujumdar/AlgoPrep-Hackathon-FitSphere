Squats

Exercise Description

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, barbell resting on your upper back. Lower your body by bending your knees and hips until your thighs are parallel to the floor, then push back up to the starting position. A good warm up excercise.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 10-15 kg dumbbells per hand

Rest time between each set: 45secs
