Russian Twists

Exercise Description

Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet lifted slightly. Lean back slightly and hold a weight or medicine ball with both hands. Rotate your torso to the right, bringing the weight towards the floor beside your hip, then twist to the left.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 30-45 reps each side

Weights: 7-10kg

Rest time between each set: 45secs
