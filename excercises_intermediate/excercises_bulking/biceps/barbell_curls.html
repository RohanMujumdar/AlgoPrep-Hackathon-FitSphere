Barbell Curls

Exercise Description

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell with palms facing up and hands shoulder-width apart. Curl the barbell up towards your shoulders, then slowly lower it back to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 10-15 kg plates per side of barbell

Rest time between each set: 45secs
