Kettlebell Snatches

Exercise Description

Kettlebell snatches engage the whole body. Swing the kettlebell between your legs, then explosively lift it overhead in one smooth motion. Lower it back down and repeat.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Weights: 10-15 kg

Rest time between each set: 30secs
