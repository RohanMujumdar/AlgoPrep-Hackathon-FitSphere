Bulgarian Split Squats

Exercise Description

Bulgarian split squats target the quads and glutes. Stand a few feet in front of a bench, place one foot behind you on the bench, and lower your body until your front thigh is parallel to the floor. Push back up to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 15-20 reps per leg

Weights: 10-15 kg per hand

Rest time between each set: 30secs
