Plyometric Push-Ups

Exercise Description

Plyometric push-ups are an explosive exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Perform a standard push-up, but push off the ground with enough force to lift your hands off the floor. Land softly and go directly into the next rep.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Rest time between each set: 30secs
