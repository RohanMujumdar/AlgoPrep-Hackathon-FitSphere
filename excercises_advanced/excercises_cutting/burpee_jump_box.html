Burpee Jump Box

Exercise Description

Burpee jump box combines a burpee with a box jump for a full-body workout. Begin in a standing position, perform a burpee by dropping into a push-up position and then jumping back up. From the standing position, jump onto a box or platform, then step or jump back down.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest time between each set: 30secs
