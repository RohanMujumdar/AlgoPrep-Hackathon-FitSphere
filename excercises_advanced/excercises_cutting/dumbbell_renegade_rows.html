Dumbbell Renegade Rows

Exercise Description

Dumbbell renegade rows target the back, core, and arms. Start in a high plank position with a dumbbell in each hand. Row one dumbbell towards your hip while stabilizing your body with the other arm. Alternate sides, maintaining a strong core throughout.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side

Weights: 7-10 kg per hand

Rest time between each set: 30secs
