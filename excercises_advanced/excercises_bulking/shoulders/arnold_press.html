Arnold Press

Exercise Description

Sit on a bench with back support, holding dumbbells in front of your shoulders, palms facing you. Press the dumbbells overhead while rotating your palms to face forward, then lower them back to the starting position with control.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 7-10kg dumbbell per hand

Rest time between each set: 45secs
