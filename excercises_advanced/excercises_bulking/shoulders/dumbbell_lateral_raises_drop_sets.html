Dumbbell Lateral Raises With Drop Sets

Exercise Description

Stand with your feet hip-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at your sides. Lift the dumbbells out to the sides until they are level with your shoulders, then lower them back down slowly.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 7-10kg dumbbell per hand

Rest time between each set: 45secs
