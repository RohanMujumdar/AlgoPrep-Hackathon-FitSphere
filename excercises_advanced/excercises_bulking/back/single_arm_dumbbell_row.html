Single Arm Dumbbell Rows With Extreme Range of Motion

Exercise Description

Place one knee and hand on a bench for support, holding a dumbbell in the opposite hand. Allow the dumbbell to hang fully extended, then pull it up to your side, achieving a full range of motion before lowering it back down.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Weights: 10-15kg dumbbell

Rest time between each set: 45secs
