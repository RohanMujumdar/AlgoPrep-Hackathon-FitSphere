Weighted Pull-Ups

Exercise Description

Attach a weight belt or hold a dumbbell between your legs. Hang from a pull-up bar with a grip slightly wider than shoulder-width. Pull your body up until your chin is above the bar, then lower yourself back down.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Weights: 10kg plate

Rest time between each set: 45secs
