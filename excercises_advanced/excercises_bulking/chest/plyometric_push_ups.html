Plyometric Push-Ups

Exercise Description

Start in a standard push-up position. Lower your body towards the floor, then explosively push up, allowing your hands to leave the ground. Land softly and immediately go into the next rep. A good warm-up excercise.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Rest time between each set: 45secs
