Single Arm Dumbbell Bench Press

Exercise Description

Lie on a bench with one dumbbell in one hand. Keep the other arm extended at your side or place it on your torso for stability. Press the dumbbell up from chest level to arm's length, then lower it back down.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm

Weights: 10kg

Rest time between each set: 45secs
