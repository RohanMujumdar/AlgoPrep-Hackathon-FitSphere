Rajakapotasana

Exercise Description

Begin in a pigeon pose with one leg forward and the other leg extended back. Bend your back leg and reach for your foot with both hands. Arch your back and lift your chest towards the ceiling, aiming to touch your foot with your hands.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Rest time between each set: 45secs
