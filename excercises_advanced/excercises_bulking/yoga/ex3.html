Urdhva Dhanurasana

Exercise Description

Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands beside your ears, fingers pointing towards your shoulders. Push through your feet and hands to lift your chest and hips off the ground into a full backbend.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Rest time between each set: 45secs
