Hammer Curls With Heavy Weights

Exercise Description

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding dumbbells at your sides with a neutral grip (palms facing each other). Curl the dumbbells up towards your shoulders while keeping your elbows close to your body, then lower them back down slowly.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per hand

Weights: 10-15 kg dumbbells per hand

Rest time between each set: 1min
