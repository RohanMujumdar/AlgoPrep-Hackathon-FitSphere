Bicep Curls With Heavy Weights

Exercise Description

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell or dumbbells with an underhand grip. Curl the weights up towards your shoulders, fully contracting your biceps, then lower them back down slowly.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Weights: 10-15 kg dumbbells per hand

Rest time between each set: 45secs
