Tricep Push Down With Heavier Weights

Exercise Description

Stand facing a cable machine with the handle attachment at the high setting. Grip the handle with both hands, elbows bent at 90 degrees. Push the handle downward until your arms are fully extended, then slowly return to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Weights: 20-25kg

Rest time between each set: 45secs
