Burpees

Exercise Description

Burpees are a high-intensity exercise that works the entire body. Start in a standing position, drop into a squat, kick your feet back into a plank, jump your feet back to the squat position, and then jump up.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest time between each set: 30secs
