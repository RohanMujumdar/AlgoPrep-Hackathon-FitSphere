Body Weight Squats

Exercise Description

Bodyweight squats are a great way to build strength and endurance in your legs and lower body. To perform a bodyweight squat, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, keep your chest up, and squat down by bending your knees and hips, then return to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Rest time between each set: 30secs
