Seated Dumbell Press

Exercise Description

The seated dumbbell press targets the shoulders and triceps. Sit on a bench with back support and hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height. Press the dumbbells overhead until your arms are fully extended, then lower them back to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 8-12 reps, weights: 2-5 kg

Rest time between each set: 30secs
