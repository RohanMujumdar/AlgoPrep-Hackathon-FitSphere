Bird Dog

Exercise Description

The bird dog exercise targets the core, lower back, and glutes. Start on all fours with your hands directly under your shoulders and knees under your hips. Extend one arm forward and the opposite leg backward, keeping your body in a straight line. Return to the starting position and switch sides.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-15 reps per side

Rest time between each set: 30secs
