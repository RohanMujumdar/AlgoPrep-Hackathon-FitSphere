Standing Dumbell Curls

Exercise Description

TStanding dumbbell curls target the biceps. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a dumbbell in each hand with your arms fully extended. Curl the weights towards your shoulders by bending your elbows, then lower them back down.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-15 reps, weights: 2-5 kg

Rest time between each set: 30secs
