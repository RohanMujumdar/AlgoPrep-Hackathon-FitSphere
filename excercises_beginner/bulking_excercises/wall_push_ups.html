Wall Push-Ups

Exercise Description

Wall push-ups are a beginner-friendly exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Stand facing a wall with your hands placed slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lean forward into the wall, then push back to the starting position.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Rest time between each set: 30secs
