Chair Squats

Exercise Description

Chair squats are a beginner-friendly exercise that strengthens the legs and glutes. Stand in front of a chair with your feet shoulder-width apart. Lower your body by bending your knees and hips as if sitting down, then rise back to the starting position without actually sitting.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Rest time between each set: 30secs
