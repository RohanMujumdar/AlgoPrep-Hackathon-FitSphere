Standing Calf Raises

Exercise Description

Standing calf raises target the calves. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and rise onto the balls of your feet, lifting your heels as high as possible. Lower your heels back down to the starting position. You can add weights to your hand if you want.

Reps and Sets

Beginners: 3 sets of 12-15 reps, weights: 2-5 kg

Rest time between each set: 30secs
